I am a system, harddware security and program analysis researcher. I am working as a researcher at Inria (Grenoble, France), in the Corse team.

Previously, I was researcher at the University of Lille, France. I am associate professor in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the Lebanese University, Lebanon. Prior to that I was assistant professor in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Fahad Bin Sultan University, Saudi Arabia. Before that, I worked as a staff software engineering at IBM in Austin, TX and Hudson Valley Research Park, NY. I obtained my PhD from the American University of Beirut in October 2011.

Among other topics, I am interested in processor microarchitecutre, machine learning, binary analysis, formal and functional verification, microarchitectural covert and side channels in commodity computers and servers, and reverse-engineering processor parts.
